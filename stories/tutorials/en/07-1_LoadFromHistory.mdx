# Loading from History

You can resume work from your previous editing session.
The history is saved in your browser, so don't worry if you forget to download the file.

## When to Use This Method

- ✅ **Continue your previous editing session**
- ✅ **Resume editing on another day**
- ✅ **Accidentally forgot to download**

## Steps

### 1. Choose Loading Method

Select the "**Load History**" tab at the top of the dialog.

> 💡 **Convenient Feature**: If you load the same folder from a previously edited zip file,
> your previous editing data is automatically selected. You can start right where you left off.

### 2. Select History

Choose the history you want to continue editing from the select box.

<div style={{marginTop: '16px', marginBottom: '16px', border: '1px solid #ddd', borderRadius: '8px', overflow: 'hidden', height: '600px'}}>

  />
</div>

Displayed information:
- **Filename**: Name of the zip file
- **Folder name**: Which folder's oto.ini
- **Last modified**: Date and time of last edit

### 3. Click "OK" to Resume Editing

Select the history you want to continue and tap "OK".
Your previous edits will be loaded and you'll proceed to the editing screen.

## About History Storage

### Where It's Saved

History is saved in your browser's local storage.

- ✅ Saved on your device, so works offline too
- ✅ Not sent externally as personal information

### Storage Duration

History remains as long as you don't clear your browser data.

> ⚠️ **Note**: History may be deleted in these cases:
> - When you clear browser history/cache
> - When using private browsing (incognito mode)
> - When device storage is insufficient

### Backup Recommendation

For important oto.ini files, don't rely only on history - download them regularly.

## Managing Multiple Voicebanks

History for different folders of the same voicebank are saved separately.

Example:
- `voicebank/normal/` history
- `voicebank/whisper/` history
- `voicebank/append/` history

Each is managed independently, so there's no risk of mixing them up.

## If History is Not Found

If history doesn't appear, check the following:

1. **Same browser and device?**
   - History isn't shared across devices or browsers
   
2. **Cleared browser data?**
   - Clearing history/cache also deletes editing history

3. **Using private browsing?**
   - Incognito mode doesn't save history
