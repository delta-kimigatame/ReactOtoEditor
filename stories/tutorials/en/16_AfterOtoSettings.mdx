# After Oto Settings

Oto settings complete! Congratulations!

Here's what to do after finishing oto settings.

## Making It Usable in UTAU

To actually use your edited oto settings in UTAU, you need other tools.

### <img src="icons/gakuya.png" alt="GAKUYA" width="30" /> About GAKUYA

**GAKUYA** is LABERU's sister app.
It creates files needed for voicebank distribution (`character.txt`, etc.).

After editing oto settings in LABERU, complete your voicebank with GAKUYA.

- [GAKUYA Official Site](https://k-uta.jp/gakuya/)

> 💡 **Hint**: Combining LABERU and GAKUYA lets you create voicebanks without a computer.

**What you can do in GAKUYA:**
- Create `character.txt`
- Create `readme.txt`
- Set voicebank images
- Create distribution zip file

## Try Your Voicebank

### If You Have UTAU

If you have UTAU on your computer, try this:

1. **Download zip file** from LABERU
2. Add `character.txt` etc. with GAKUYA
3. Load voicebank in UTAU and make it sing

### If You Don't Have UTAU

If you don't have a computer or UTAU, use **utalet**.

#### <img src="icons/utalet.png" alt="utalet" width="30" /> About utalet

**utalet** is UTAU that runs in browsers.
It's LABERU's sister app, so compatibility is excellent.

- [utalet Official Site](https://k-uta.jp/utalet/)

**utalet features:**
- No installation needed
- Works on smartphones and tablets
- Loads voicebanks made with LABERU directly

> 💡 **Hint**: You can do everything on just a smartphone: oto settings in LABERU → complete voicebank in GAKUYA → make it sing in utalet!

## Publishing Your Voicebank

Share your completed voicebank with others.

GAKUYA's tutorial explains publication methods in detail.

> 💡 **Hint**: After completing oto settings in LABERU, add `character.txt` and terms of use in GAKUYA to complete your voicebank.
